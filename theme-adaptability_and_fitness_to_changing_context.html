The Fragile Hierarchy
(Block)
The Fragile Hierarchy

Description: Rigid hierarchies hinder innovation and adaptation.

Problem: Rigid hierarchies become brittle when leaders and established structures resist change and dissent.

Solution: Highlights the risk of centralized power that stifles innovation and impedes adaptive change.

The Vanity Metric Trap
(Block)
The Vanity Metric Trap

Description: Overemphasis on superficial metrics obscures real impact.

Problem: Focusing on superficial, quantifiable metrics can obscure deeper systemic issues and impede meaningful progress.

Solution: Warns against mistaking vanity metrics for genuine impact and urges a focus on substantive outcomes.

Resources: nan
Related Patterns:
Reflect, Adapt, Iterate, Story as Feedback
Suppressing Dissent
(Block)
Suppressing Dissent

Description: Silencing dissent prevents the healthy challenge necessary for adaptation.

Problem: Treating internal disagreement as disruptive silences critical voices and stifles innovation.

Solution: Marginalizing dissent prevents adaptive governance; open dialogue is needed to challenge and improve systems.

Accountability as Care
(Unblock)
Accountability as Care

Description: Reframes accountability as a supportive, caring practice.

Problem: Traditional, punitive accountability models can damage relationships and inhibit collaboration.

Solution: Reframe accountability as an act of care—emphasizing shared values and mutual responsibility—to foster a supportive, growth-oriented environment.

Resources: nan
Related Patterns:
Reflect, Adapt, Iterate, Story as Feedback, From Ownership to Stewardship
From Ownership to Stewardship
(Unblock)
From Ownership to Stewardship

Description: Shifts focus from individual ownership to collective stewardship.

Problem: When work is “owned” by individuals, it can lead to silos and burnout rather than collective progress.

Solution: Promote a stewardship model where responsibility is shared, encouraging collaboration and a sense of collective ownership over outcomes.

Resources: nan
Related Patterns:
Accountability as Care, Reflect, Adapt, Iterate, Rest as Governance
